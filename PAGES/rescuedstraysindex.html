RESCUED STRAYS

At our affiliated shelters, numerous rescues await their beloved homes. Various of our adoptable pets have been saved from dire situations such as local pounds or abusive environments. Fortunately, they have all been taken care of their medical condition and are just waiting for the perfect timing to be adopted!


With adoption, you will play a major role in our FURfriends’ FUR-fect life. You are also aiding in freeing up more space in our affiliated shelters, enabling them to rescue more stray animals out there that needs saving.


With the help of FurFriend Finder, there are currently 9 rescued dogs and cats that are under the shelters' safekeeping. Together, we can help these lovable furfriends achieve the life they deserve, one loving home at a time.
